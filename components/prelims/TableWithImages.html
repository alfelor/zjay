Baler, Aurora

A Historical and Beautiful Destination


Baler is a coastal town located in the province of Aurora, Philippines. It sits along the eastern coast of Luzon, facing the Pacific Ocean. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, Baler is famous for its beaches, stunning rock formations, and lush mountains, making it a favorite destination for surfers and nature lovers.

Baler has a rich history dating back to the Spanish colonial period. One of its most significant historical events is the Siege of Baler (1898-1899), where Spanish soldiers took refuge in the town's church for nearly a year. The town was officially founded in 1609 and later became the capital of Aurora province. Today, Baler is recognized for its historical landmarks, including the Baler Church and Museo de Baler.
